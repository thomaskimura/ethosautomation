Our Story

The founding members of Ethos have come from three distinct fields of Automation Engineering/Design, Project Management, and Controls. We feel with this collaborated background coming together, we will be able to deliver a reliable & innovative turn key solution for our customers needs. Through the vast understanding of the manufacturing industry, strong design background and extensive floor experience, we are able to specialize in, current process modifications based on product changes or improvements and new product implementations.

Automated Machines

With the capability to design, build and integrate machines, Ethos Automation provides an all-encompassing solution to service our clients. Our strength is 3D CAD design, paired with client care and integration sets us apart. All technical programming is done in house by the professional team utilizing the latest technology, ensuring that we find the most effective solu- tion for our clients.

Retrofits and Automation Services

Ethos Automation offers automation services to improve and help integrate new technology for your business. Included in this diverse line-up of services is robot programming, programma- ble logic control programming, cycle time improvements, tooling design, engineering optimiza- tion including process and material flow and machine retrofits. Through machine retrofits, Ethos Automation works to upgrade outdated or lesser functioning machinery to perform more efficiently and do more. In turn, this helps your business more effectively.

Automated Manufacturing and Prototyping

Ethos Automation offers a full-service manufacturing and prototyping experience, providing a complete solution from start to finish. Beginning with an audit of the functionality of your design or part, Ethos Automation will spend time with your time to determine improvements that can be made. Developmental feedback will be provided, alongside possible improvement ideas to ensure your product is ready for manufacturing. From the start of manufacturing to the completion of any given part, Ethos Automation works diligently with our clients to ensure the highest functioning result is achieved. Our services include, but are not limited to, robotic welding, robotic plasma cutting, CNC machining and metal prototype work.
